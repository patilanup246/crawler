  • Search for a Lawyer or Law Practice

The LSRA’s integrated search function lists all lawyers, individuals and law practice entities registered with the LSRA and/or the Supreme Court of Singapore. As the search results are based on the applications submitted to the LSRA and/or the Supreme Court of Singapore, the Ministry of Law does not make any warranty or representation as to their accuracy, completeness or reliability. Please also refer to our Terms of Use.

To update the particulars of:
  • A Singapore solicitor practising in a Singapore law practice – Please file a Notice of Change of Particulars via the Supreme Court’s eLitigation.
  • An individual registered with the LSRA (i.e. a Singapore solicitor practising in a foreign law practice entity, a foreign lawyer, or a regulated non-practitioner) – Please submit an application to update their Certificate of Registration via LSRA e-Services.
  • A law practice entity – Please submit an application to update its licence via LSRA e-Services.

Please visit our website for more information on the types of law practice entities and lawyer registrations.
