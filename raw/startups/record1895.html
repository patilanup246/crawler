Description
SIIX-AGT MEDTECH is a provider of robotics for healthcare. They specialise in service and surgical robots clinical application, design and development.

The company has created a power-assisted technology for hospitals beds, earning them first prize in Singapore's National Healthcare Group's Innovation Supplier Award in 2014.
